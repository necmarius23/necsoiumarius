Cloud.NET

I’ve always found myself interested in how data works and how can be stored in order to be safe and also accessible at any time of the day. With that being said there’s no much of a surprise that cloud storage caught my eye, since this method is accessible to everyone. When I made this project I kept in mind that cloud storage should be a method that could be used by any type of user, not only by developers or computer science employees, but by any person who wants to keep his files safe.

From scratch to sketch

At the beginning of this project I tried to focus on two important things: the first one was to make the design as clean and easy to understand as possible so any client should be able to make an idea of how to use the storage. The other was to make it modern, in order to catch the eye of the viewer. So I took a piece of paper, my drawing tools and I started sketching, I also found a way to lay the content in order to acquintance the user with the position of the elements so they would have a natural experience.

And the finished product

At last, I had to add the "finishing touch" for the product, picking the right colors and the right images, so the project could look and feel natural.
