Xplore the wildlife of Africa!

As the main title of the application states “Xplore the wildlife of Africa”. This project is focused in exploring one of the greatest and diverse wildlife our planet ever knew, Africa. This idea was driven to me from one of my biggest concerns, the danger of pollution over nature, whether we talk about climate change or poaching. The goal was to design an application that could allow people to learn about Africa through a fun an interactive method, where the user would be allowed to choose the region he wants to explore.

From scratch to sketch

Since the goal of this project was a mobile application I chose to focus on making the design as interactive as possible for the user. I chose to add a map of Africa so that any user could be able to select the region he wants to explore. Next step was thinking how to give the client the opportunity of choosing between flora and fauna, how to allow him to easily choose what part of the wildlife wants to explore. I also tried to give the user the option to search by himself a region or a certain fauna through the search bar which I positioned beside the menu button.

And the finished product

The goal of this project was to bring awareness over the wildlife of Africa. I chose to keep the design simple and interactive for the user.
