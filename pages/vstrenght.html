Vstrenght, your number one fitness app!

Personally, I think sport is very important for a healthy and happy life. Practicing sports, like going to gym, or playing a certain game (football, basketball, handball, etc) will help your body feel better, allowing you to sleep better, more energy and a better health overall. This inspired me to make this project, where I designed a mobile application with the purpose of helping others getting in shape, offering them workouts based on their needs, body type, and a method of measuring how their diet works.

From scratch to sketch

At the beginning of this project I had to think about how the application should be divided based on their needs. I had to find a way to allow the user to choose what he wants. If the client wants to lose weight, the application should be designed in such way that it would be easy to navigate and choose the option you want. And the finished product
