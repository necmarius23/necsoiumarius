From scratch to sketch

Before starting sketching I had to make some important decisions such as how should I lay the content in order to be both pleasant and easy to understand, what style should my illustrations have? What colors should I use to describe both the familiar atmosphere and professionalism of the art group? I also had to keep in mind that the website would be art themed and all the illustrations should fit that theme. The major UX challenge was making the gallery easy to find and easy to access and understand by all users.
